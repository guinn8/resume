Vipond Controls is a systems integrator specializing in innovative and cost effective industrial controls for the energy industry. We currently have an opportunity for a junior/intermediate controls engineer.

The successful applicant will be responsible for the complete integration of PLCs, RTUs, and HMIs into a single tool that our clients use to help reduce costs and increase their operating efficiency. The successful candidate should have experience in a wide variety of technologies, like PLC programming, HMI design, visual basic programming, and web site/database design.
The ideal applicant will thrive on technical challenges, have strong programming skills, good interpersonal skills, and have a technology, engineering, or computer science degree.
